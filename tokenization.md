# Tokenization

## What is tokenization?
Tokenization is the process of breaking text into smaller units called tokens. Large language models process tokens rather than raw text.

## Why does tokenization matter?

- Context window usage
- Model performance
- Inference cost
- Multilingual NLP
- Morphologically rich languages
