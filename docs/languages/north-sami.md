# North Sámi

## AI engineering profile

North Sámi is an Indigenous Uralic language spoken across northern Norway, Sweden, Finland, and Russia.

Although it has comparatively limited digital resources, North Sámi represents one of the most important case studies for multilingual AI because it combines rich morphology, multilingual public-service requirements, cross-border language technology, and Indigenous language support.

Within the Multilingual Benchmark Explorer, North Sámi serves as the flagship language for investigating Indigenous language technology and multilingual AI systems that support smaller language communities.

---

## Why North Sámi is an important benchmark

North Sámi provides an outstanding benchmark because it combines:

- rich agglutinative morphology
- comparatively limited digital resources
- multilingual public-service requirements
- cross-border language use
- active language revitalization efforts
- growing investment in language technology

Rather than representing a limitation, these characteristics make North Sámi an ideal language for evaluating how multilingual AI systems perform outside the world's largest languages.

North Sámi serves as the repository's flagship Indigenous language.
