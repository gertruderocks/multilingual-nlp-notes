# Inari Sámi

## AI engineering profile

Inari Sámi is an Indigenous Uralic language spoken exclusively in Finland and one of the country's three officially recognized Sámi languages.

Although comparatively limited in digital resources, Inari Sámi represents an exceptional case study for multilingual AI because it combines Indigenous language technology, language revitalization, educational AI, and multilingual public services within a single national context.

Within the Multilingual Benchmark Explorer, Inari Sámi serves as the flagship case study for investigating AI support for a single-country Indigenous language community.

---

## Why Inari Sámi is an important benchmark

Inari Sámi provides an outstanding benchmark because it combines:

- rich agglutinative morphology
- comparatively limited digital resources
- active language revitalization
- educational innovation
- multilingual public services
- well-defined language community

Rather than representing a limitation, these characteristics make Inari Sámi an ideal language for investigating how multilingual AI systems can support community-driven language technology.
