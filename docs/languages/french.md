# French

## AI engineering profile

French is one of the world's most important high-resource languages for multilingual AI engineering.

Its extensive digital resources, broad international use, and prominent role in multilingual benchmark datasets make French an essential comparison language for tokenization, embeddings, retrieval, translation, and multilingual evaluation.

Within the Multilingual Benchmark Explorer, French serves as a major secondary research language and an important benchmark for cross-lingual AI systems.

---

## Why French is an important benchmark

French provides an excellent benchmark because it combines:

- extensive multilingual datasets
- strong NLP tooling
- global usage across multiple continents
- significant influence on many neighboring languages
- high-quality machine translation resources

French is valuable both as a benchmark language and as a historical source of lexical influence across Europe.

## Morphology

French exhibits moderate fusional morphology.

Characteristics include:

- grammatical gender
- verb conjugation
- derivational morphology
- relatively fixed word order

Research questions:

- How does French morphology compare with Germanic languages?
- Which multilingual embeddings best represent French?
