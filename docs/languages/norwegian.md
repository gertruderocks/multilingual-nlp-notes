# Norwegian

## AI engineering profile

Norwegian is a North Germanic language that provides an excellent benchmark for multilingual AI systems in the Nordic region.

Its close relationship with Swedish and Danish, strong digital language resources, and important role in multilingual public services make Norwegian especially valuable for investigating tokenization, embeddings, retrieval, multilingual evaluation, and language contact.

Within the Multilingual Benchmark Explorer, Norwegian serves as the flagship language for studying multilingual AI in the Nordic region and as a primary comparison language for North Sámi.

---

## Why Norwegian is an important benchmark

Norwegian provides an excellent benchmark because it combines:

- strong digital language resources
- close relationships with Swedish and Danish
- multilingual public-sector language technology
- support for Indigenous languages
- high-quality NLP tools
- extensive multilingual corpora

This makes Norwegian valuable for investigating multilingual AI within both Germanic languages and multilingual public-service settings.
