# Georgian

## AI engineering profile

Georgian is the flagship representative of the Kartvelian language family within the Multilingual Benchmark Explorer.

Its unique writing system, rich morphology, extensive literary tradition, and long history of multilingual interaction in the Caucasus make Georgian an outstanding benchmark for investigating multilingual AI beyond the Indo-European and Uralic language families.

Within this repository, Georgian serves as the flagship language for studying multilingual AI across distinct language families and writing systems.

---

## Why Georgian is an important benchmark

Georgian provides an outstanding benchmark because it combines:

- a unique writing system
- rich morphology
- strong national language resources
- multilingual regional context
- extensive historical documentation
- valuable cross-family comparisons

Rather than fitting neatly into the Indo-European or Uralic framework, Georgian provides an opportunity to evaluate how multilingual AI generalizes across fundamentally different linguistic systems.
