# Dutch

## Why Dutch matters for AI

Dutch is a high-resource West Germanic language that provides an excellent benchmark for multilingual AI systems. It is closely related to English and German, making it useful for studying cross-lingual transfer, multilingual embeddings, retrieval, and machine translation.

Within this repository, Dutch also serves as the primary language for building the Dutch Study Agent, allowing experiments to connect language learning, multilingual NLP, and AI-assisted educational tools.
